New Blood Team


Welcome to the home of team New Blood!

Brought together by the initiative of Oleg (PreZandy) and Beto (kurumim), we’re a happy crew of chess enthusiasts who love playing in FICS TeamLeague and sharing chess knowledge and experiences. Combining long-time and new friends, all dedicated and trustworthy, we started as a brand new team in Season 71 (April-June 2018) and always look forward to more action on the board!

Organised in different squads for each season, we work to address the interests of every Bloodie and to improve both as players and as a team, always supporting and cheering on one another!
