New Blood Team


Should you see this page, this means you have received a personal invitation to the team.

Our addiction is chess, our location is FICS, our aim is to bring New Blood into TeamLeague, which is the best place for amateur chess teams all over the world to compete as well as to share experience and contribute to chess.


The "New Blood" Team was established in February 2018 in the wake of friendly talks between Beto (kurumim) from Brazil and Oleg (PreZandy) from Russia. Beto has been holding a vast record in Teamleague so far while Oleg has just entered TeamLeague in Season 70. 

We are targeting Teamleague Season 71 to come up with two squads (leaded by Beto and Oleg, respectively) full of trustworthy and open-minded teammates who are entering TeamLeague ready to provide the event with a new feel for the game and unique insight into the pawn and other pretty damn smart structures, he he :)

We believe that this far-reaching strategic approach leads us as a team to major achievements while furthering both sonority and diversity amongst the TeamLeague community, which is a great result by itself.

Should you share the same feeling and view — cheers, and welcome on board!
