New Blood Team


This section is intended for reporting the results and performance of the team. To be updated since TL Season 71.

In the meantime, we will share here some remarkable games of the current Teamleague Season 70 and previous TL Seasons played by the founders of the team as well by other Teamleaguers.

To navigate easily a game, you can use cursor arrow keys. First, give a focus to a game by clicking on a game frame. Now you can use /left/ and /right/ to browse the game, while /up/ and /down/ to browse the comments of the audience.

P.S. If the contents of a game frame is not shown, you should check your browser security settings. Click on the notification sign (in Firefox it is in the beginning of the URL address bar, in Chrome - in the end of the URL address bar, in IE you would probably see the popup) and allow unsecured content.
